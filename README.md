# 🎭 Isabella's Manga: The Chronicles of the Cosmos 🌌

This repository documents the lore, characters, and story arcs for Isabella's original manga series.

---

## 👥 Main Characters & Profiles

The main group consists of four friends: two girls (**Isa** & **Mila**) and two boys (**Elyseo** & **Evan**).

### 💫 Isa (The Universal Sovereign)
- **Gender:** Female
- **Power:** **Universe Powers (Black Hole & Everything in the Universe)**. The strongest character in the series.
- **Family Lore:**
  - **Mother:** Eve (Tragically died in an accident).
  - **Father:** Josh (Tragically died in an accident).
  - **Siblings:** 
    - **Amilia:** Younger sister.
    - **Vera:** Older sister who is very mean and abandoned Isa when she was little.
- **Current Conflict:** Vera has become a "bad person," leading to intense future battles between the sisters.

### ⚡ Mila (The Thunderstorm)
- **Gender:** Female
- **Power:** **Thunder Powers**. Very special and powerful.
- **Best Friend:** Isa.
- **Family Lore:**
  - **Mother:** Clara (She is currently sick).
  - **Father:** Richard (Abusive).
- **Backstory:** Despite her difficult home life, she grew up incredibly strong and resilient.

### 🔥 Elyseo (The Scorching Soul)
- **Gender:** Male
- **Power:** **Fire Powers**. Extremely powerful.
- **Family Lore:**
  - **Mother:** Grace (Deceased).
  - **Father:** Victor (Cold, mean, and abandoned Elyseo to force him to become "stronger").
- **Backstory:** Just like Isa, Elyseo's power grew from a place of abandonment and hardship.

### ✨ Evan (The Guardian's Legacy)
- **Gender:** Male
- **Power:** [TBD]
- **Family Lore:**
  - **Mother:** Amber (Mother of Evan who left him).
  - **Father:** [Unnamed] (Left Amber, causing her to leave Evan).
  - **Guardian:** **Grandma Aurelia** stepped in to raise him.
- **Backstory:** Thanks to Aurelia's guidance, Evan became a good person and remarkably strong.

## 💀 The Villains (The Five Strongest)

These are the most powerful adversaries our heroes face. Each has a dark past and dangerous abilities.

### 🕸️ Kiki
- **Gender:** Female
- **Power:** **Void String Powers**.
- **Appearance:** Short hair.
- **Personality:** "Happy but evil"—dark-sided and sinister.
- **Backstory:** Killed her own parents because they were abusive and tried to kill her. She is remarkably strong.

### 🤖 Mason
- **Gender:** Male
- **Power:** **Tech Powers (Intelligence & Robotics)**.
- **Appearance:** Curly hair. Unlike others, he has no mark or colored hair; he is a human who chose to be evil.
- **Backstory:** His parents are alive but terrified of him. He uses his superior intelligence as his primary weapon.

### 🐍 Seth
- **Gender:** Male
- **Power:** **Venom Power**.
- **Personality:** Shy and introverted. However, if he becomes mad or frustrated in battle, he loses his mind and becomes incredibly strong.
- **Backstory:** His mother died, and his father cared for him until Seth turned bad for mysterious reasons.

### 👁️ Holly
- **Gender:** Female
- **Power:** **Dream Power (Illusion & Silence)**.
- **Appearance:** Has a **Third Eye** in her forehead.
- **Backstory:** Her parents are unknown. She was not an orphan, but her origin is a mystery. When she needs help, she opens her third eye to fool people in their dreams.

### 🦁 Silence
- **Gender:** Male
- **Power:** **Beast Power (Animal Control & Creation)**.
- **Appearance:** Serious expression, spiky hair.
- **Backstory:** His parents died sadly in front of him when he was a child.
- **Abilities:** He can control animals or even create them in seconds during battle to fight for him.

## ⚔️ Key Story Arcs & Lore
- **The Tragedy of the Past:** Every character carries a "sad story" marked by loss, abuse, or abandonment.
- **The Rift:** The primary rivalry between Isa and her older sister, Vera.
- **The Tone:** While the bond between friends is strong, the world is dangerous, featuring "blood scenes" and high-stakes battles.
